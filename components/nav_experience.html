Wine and food

tasting

Santorini is home to some truly unique wines, not only because of its volcanic soil and dry climate, but because the island's vineyards never suffered the phylloxera blight that transformed European wine making. The unique climate and volcanic terrain make them some of the most distinctive wines of Greece.

Enjoy a unique sailing

experience

Do not leave Santorini unless you have experienced a boat tour! Its greatest asset is that you can face the impressive caldera and its picturesque villages from the sea. In addition, you will swim in the crystal-clear waters of the most popular beaches of the island, like the Red Beach and the White Beach, as well as in the therapeutic waters of the volcano's hot springs. Whichever boat tour you choose, you will see the volcano up close and walk on it! What is more, some boat tours provide barbeque meals on board and unlimited local wine and beers, or even special cocktails! There are sunset options too, for those who want to admire the sunset of Santorini on board! Whether you are on a honeymoon with your other half or a large group of friends looking for a unique party onboard or a family, then a private boat tour is the perfect choice for your special occasion!
